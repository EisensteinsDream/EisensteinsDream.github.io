Autodrawings

I do not consider myself an adept illustrator. I still try to make one 'autodrawing' a day. The lack of expectations allows me to approach these with no filter. I suppose you can consider it pure surrealism. Nothing in these have any intrinsic meaning, nor are they intended to resemble anything. Intention was not a factor in their creation at all.

These are made mostly with a cheap pen. Some involve a felt pen. All are drawn on regular notebook paper. I do not electronically process them at all after scanning. Feel free to view them, if you like.
